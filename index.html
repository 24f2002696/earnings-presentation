Financial Formulas

Compound Interest Formula:

\[A = P\left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^{nt}\]

Net Present Value:

\[NPV = \sum_{t=0}^{n} \frac{C_t}{(1+r)^t}\]

Return on Investment:

\[ROI = \frac{Gain - Cost}{Cost} \times 100\%\]
